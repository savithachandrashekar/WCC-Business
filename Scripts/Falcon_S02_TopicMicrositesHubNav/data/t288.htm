How to solve HDMI problems

There's no sound coming from the speakers in the monitor or television connected via an HDMI cable to your PC.

  1. Follow the steps in this video for basic HDML audio troubleshooting.

  2. If you still have problems, try the steps in Advanced HDMI Display and Sound Troubleshooting.

  3. If this doesn't solve your problem follow the audio troubleshooting steps in I have no sound or my sound is not working on this page.

  4. The HP Support Communities may have information, or you can ask the experts directly.
