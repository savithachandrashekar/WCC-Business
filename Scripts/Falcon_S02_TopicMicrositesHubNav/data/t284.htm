Beats drivers and Audio Console

There are no specific drivers for the Beats audio and speakers built into your laptop, so you need to use the Windows update tool to ensure you have the correct audio drivers.

This video contains general instructions for checking if your WIndows 10 drivers are up to date.

If you're looking for a specific driver, visit the Software Download Page to select your product, and then download the driver you're looking for.

If you're having problems with a Beats headset, go to the Beats support website.

The HP Support Communities may have information, or you can ask the experts directly.
